## Compliance & Risk Checklist
- ☐ No sensitive data (PII, financial, health) entered into non-approved tools
- ☐ Industry rules considered (e.g., **HIPAA** for healthcare, financial regulations, client confidentiality)
- ☐ Human review step defined and assigned
- ☐ Vendor security/privacy terms reviewed
- ☐ Cost ceiling set to avoid surprise AI/cloud bills (light FinOps)
