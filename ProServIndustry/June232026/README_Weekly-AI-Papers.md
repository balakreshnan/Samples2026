## How this was built

Pulled live from arXiv listings (cs.CL / cs.AI / cs.LG / cs.IR / cs.CV / cs.CR) and weekly
aggregators (ArXiv TLDR, arxiv-daily, Let's Data Science, The AI Report) on June 23, 2026.

**Caveats worth knowing:**
- arXiv publishes 600+ cs.CL papers/week alone, so this is a *representative wide sweep*, not a census.
- arXiv IDs map only approximately to dates within the month; a few high-visibility items from June 3–14 are included and clearly labeled **"just before the window"** for context.
- Headline numbers are as reported by the papers and have not been independently reproduced.
